FX Hedging Strategy Knowledge Base

1. Strategy Overview
The FX Hedging Strategy is a confluence-based technical model designed to quantify the risk of a trend reversal against a user's current market position (Long or Short). It calculates a "Risk Score" from 0 to 100, where a higher score indicates a higher probability of reversal and thus a greater need to hedge.

2. Scoring System (0-100)
The total score is the sum of points from four key technical indicators. The score is normalized to 100 based on the active indicators.

   - Trend (30 points): Uses Exponential Moving Average (EMA) and Simple Moving Average (SMA).
   - Momentum (30 points): Uses Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD).
   - Volatility (25 points): Uses Bollinger Bands (BB).
   - RSI (15 points): Uses Relative Strength Index (RSI).

3. Indicator Logic

   A. Trend (Weight: 30)
      - Bullish Signal: EMA is greater than SMA.
      - Bearish Signal: EMA is less than or equal to SMA.
      - Contribution: If the signal opposes your position (e.g., Bearish signal when you are Long), it adds 30 points to the Risk Score.

   B. Momentum (Weight: 30)
      - Bullish Signal: MACD line is greater than the Signal Line AND MACD is greater than 0.
      - Bearish Signal: MACD line is less than Signal Line OR MACD is less than 0.
      - Contribution: If the signal opposes your position, it adds 30 points to the Risk Score.

   C. Volatility (Weight: 25)
      - Bullish Signal: Price is greater than the Bollinger Band Upper Band OR the Middle Band is rising.
      - Bearish Signal: Price is not above Upper Band AND Middle Band is not rising.
      - Contribution: If the signal opposes your position, it adds 25 points to the Risk Score.

   D. RSI (Weight: 15)
      - Bullish Signal: RSI is greater than 50 AND RSI is rising AND RSI is less than 70 (not overbought).
      - Bearish Signal: RSI conditions are not met.
      - Contribution: If the signal opposes your position, it adds 15 points to the Risk Score.

4. Risk Score Interpretation & Actions

   - Score 80-100: Strong Hedge (75-100%)
     - High risk of reversal.
     - Action: Open a large hedging position (e.g., Sell EUR/USD if Long) to protect capital.

   - Score 60-79: Moderate Hedge (50-75%)
     - Significant risk present.
     - Action: Open a moderate hedging position.

   - Score 40-59: Partial Hedge (25-50%)
     - Some risk factors are present.
     - Action: Maintain a small protective hedge.

   - Score 0-39: No Hedge / Unwind
     - Low risk of reversal. Market is moving in your favor.
     - Action: No hedge needed; allow the position to run.

5. Dynamic Scoring
The system is dynamic. If an indicator is not selected on the chart, it is removed from the calculation, and the total score is re-normalized to 100 based on the remaining active indicators.
